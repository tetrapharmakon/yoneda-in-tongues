\tran{Te Reo Māori (ISO 639-3 mri)}{Te Lema a Yoneda}{
	Kia $\mathcal C$ he wāhanga iti, ā, ko $F$ he functor mai i tēnei wāhanga ki te wāhanga o ngā huinga. Kātahi, mō ia mea $X$ o $\mathcal C$ he bijection māori i waenga i te huinga o ngā hurihanga māori $\hom(-,X)\Rightarrow F$ me te huinga $FX$, ā, ko tēnei isomorphism ka whakamāramahia e te whakaritea
	\[
		\Big(\xi : \hom(-,X)\Rightarrow F\Big) \mapsto \xi_X(1_X)
	\]
	(ko tēnei tukanga he bijective).
}
