\tran{isiZulu (ISO 639-3 zul)}{ILema likaYoneda}{
	Ake kuthi $\mathcal C$ ibe isigaba esincane kanye no-$F$ umsebenzi kusuka kulesi sigaba kuya esigabeni samasethi. Ngakho-ke, kuyo yonke into $X$ ye-$\mathcal C$ kukhona i-bijection yemvelo phakathi kwesethi yezinguquko zemvelo $\hom(-,X)\Rightarrow F$ nesethi $FX$, futhi lo isomorphism uchazwa ngokuvumelana
	\[
		\Big(\xi : \hom(-,X)\Rightarrow F\Big) \mapsto \xi_X(1_X)
	\]
	(lo msebenzi uyi-bijective).
}
