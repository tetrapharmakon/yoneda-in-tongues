\tran{Englisc (ISO 639-3 ang)}{Yoneda lār}{
	Sīe $\mathcal C$ lytel flocc and $F$ sīe wyrcend fram þissum flocce tō flocce þāra heapa. Þonne, for ǣlcum þinge $X$ þæs flocces $\mathcal C$ is gecyndelic wrixl betwēonan þǣre heape þāra gecyndelicra hwierfinga $\hom(-,X)\Rightarrow F$ and þǣre heape $FX$, and þēos gelīcnes is āsett þurh þā wrixle
	\[
		\Big(\xi : \hom(-,X)\Rightarrow F\Big) \mapsto \xi_X(1_X)
	\]
	(þēos wyrcung is wrixlendlic).
}
