\tran{Hausa (ISO 639-3 hau)}{Lemmar Yoneda}{
	Bari $\mathcal C$ ta kasance ƙaramar rukunin kuma $F$ wani ma'aikata daga wannan rukunin zuwa rukunin saitoci. To, ga kowane abu $X$ na $\mathcal C$ akwai bijection na dabi'a tsakanin saitin canjin dabi'a $\hom(-,X)\Rightarrow F$ da saitin $FX$, kuma wannan isomorphism an bayyana shi ta hanyar daidaitawa
	\[
		\Big(\xi : \hom(-,X)\Rightarrow F\Big) \mapsto \xi_X(1_X)
	\]
	(wannan aiki bijective ne).
}
