\tran{Igbo (ISO 639-3 ibo)}{Lema Yoneda}{
	Ka $\mathcal C$ bụrụ obere nkebi ma $F$ bụrụ funktor sitere na nkebi a gaa na nkebi nke setị. Mgbe ahụ, maka ihe ọ bụla $X$ nke $\mathcal C$ enwere bijection nkịtị n'etiti setị nke mgbanwe nkịtị $\hom(-,X)\Rightarrow F$ na setị $FX$, ma isomorphism a kọwapụtara site na nkwurịta
	\[
		\Big(\xi : \hom(-,X)\Rightarrow F\Big) \mapsto \xi_X(1_X)
	\]
	(ọrụ a bụ bijective).
}
