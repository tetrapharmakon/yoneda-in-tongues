\tran{isiXhosa (ISO 639-3 xho)}{ILema kaYoneda}{
	Masithi $\mathcal C$ yiqela elincinci kunye no-$F$ isebenza ukusuka kweli qela ukuya kwiqela lamasethi. Ngoko ke, kuyo yonke into $X$ ye-$\mathcal C$ kukho i-bijection yendalo phakathi kwesethi yeenguqu zendalo $\hom(-,X)\Rightarrow F$ nesethi $FX$, kwaye lo isomorphism ichazwa ngokuvumelana
	\[
		\Big(\xi : \hom(-,X)\Rightarrow F\Big) \mapsto \xi_X(1_X)
	\]
	(lo msebenzi yi-bijective).
}
