\tran{ ĽŇĆlelo Hawai Ľi (ISO 639-3 haw)}{Ka Lema a Yoneda}{
	E $\mathcal C$ he mńĀhele li Ľili Ľi, a me $F$ he functor mai kńďia mńĀhele a i ka mńĀhele o nńĀ pŇę Ľulu. A laila, no kńďlńĀ mea $X$ o $\mathcal C$ aia he bijection kŇęlohelohe ma waena o ka pŇę Ľulu o nńĀ ho Ľololi kŇęlohelohe $\hom(-,X)\Rightarrow F$ a me ka pŇę Ľulu $FX$, a ua weheweheia kńďia isomorphism e ka pilina
	\[
		\Big(\xi : \hom(-,X)\Rightarrow F\Big) \mapsto \xi_X(1_X)
	\]
	(he bijective kńďia hana).
}
